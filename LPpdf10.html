Mouth ‚Üí oesophagus ‚Üí stomach ‚Üí small intestine ‚Üí large intestine ‚Üí anus.

Breakdown of glucose for energy.

Aerobic: with O‚ÇÇ ‚Üí CO‚ÇÇ + H‚ÇÇO + energy

Anaerobic: without O‚ÇÇ ‚Üí lactic acid/ethanol + less energy

Page 7: Human Respiratory System

Nostrils ‚Üí nasal cavity ‚Üí pharynx ‚Üí larynx ‚Üí trachea ‚Üí bronchi ‚Üí bronchioles ‚Üí alveoli.

Breathing: inhalation (diaphragm down), exhalation (diaphragm up).

Page 8: Transportation in Humans

Blood: plasma, RBC, WBC, platelets.

Heart: 4 chambers, double circulation (pulmonary, systemic).

Blood vessels: arteries, veins, capillaries.

Page 9: Transportation in Plants

Xylem: water and minerals upward.

Phloem: food bidirectional (translocation).

Page 10: Excretion

Removal of wastes.

Humans: kidneys ‚Üí nephron ‚Üí urine.

Plants: excess O‚ÇÇ, gums, resins, waste in leaves.

Page 11: Key Processes Summary

  • Respiration: aerobic/anaerobic
  • Transportation: heart, blood, xylem/phloem
  • Excretion: kidney, nephron
