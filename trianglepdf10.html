Page 3: Criteria for Similarity - AAA

If all three angles of one triangle equal to three angles of another ‚Üí similar (AAA or AA).

Strongest criterion ‚Äî two angles sufficient.

Page 4: Criteria - SSS

If all three sides of one triangle proportional to three sides of another ‚Üí similar.

Page 5: Criteria - SAS

If two sides proportional and included angle equal ‚Üí similar.

Included angle important.

Page 6: Basic Proportionality Theorem (Thales)

If a line parallel to one side of triangle intersects other two sides, then it divides those sides proportionally.
