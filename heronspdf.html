Page 4: Steps to Apply Heron‚Äôs Formula

  1. Calculate semi-perimeter s = (a + b + c)/2
  2. Compute s - a, s - b, s - c
  3. Multiply s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c)

Always check triangle inequality first.
